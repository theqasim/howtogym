Cable Lat Pulldown

How to: Position yourself sat upright and grab onto the bar to perform the movement. Hold the bar around shoulder width apart and exhale as bring the bar down towards your chest. You will notice a small retraction in your back, keep in mind this is different from pulling back completely and using your bodyweight, which is something you should not do. Make sure your reps and slow and controlled.
