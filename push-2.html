    How to: Lie back on an incline bench and make sure the bench is adjusted to 30 degrees. Unrack the bar, lower the barbell to your chest whilst avoiding flaring your elbows. Once the barbel has touched your chest, push the bar up and repeat. During the ascent and descent, control the movement and implement negatives if needed.

    • Single Arm Standing Lateral Raise

    How to: Begin this movement by standing up straight with a dumbbell in one hand holding it to your side. Slowly raise the dumbbell up and out at your side until it reaches the level of your shoulder. Hold the dumbbell at this position and then return back to the starting position. Maintain control with ascent and descent, repeat the movement for the appropriate number of repetitions then switch to your other arm.
