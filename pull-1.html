    How to: Position yourself sat upright and grab onto the bar to perform the movement. Hold the bar around shoulder width apart and exhale as bring the bar down towards your chest.     • Single Dumbbell Row

    How to: Once you're at a bench, have one leg on the bench and the other to the side of the bench. Grab the dumbbell and bring it up whilst keeping your entire arm extended and keep your back straight. Squeeze your back and shoulder muscles at the top of the movement, then return to the starting position.
